About Me

Projects

FloatRx



As a nurse, I often had to work on a specialty unit that I was not used to. These units would come with new medications and procedures that I had little knowledge of. There didn't seem to be be a central location that listed information about these medications and procedures. I would often have to learn from the other nurses, physicians, or turn to our friend Google. FloatRx is an application that sorts these medications and procedures by the unit they are most often used by in a hospital, and gives the user the ability to add those items to a personal profile. The user can then add, delete, and edit personal notes on those medications and procedures within their profile as they learn about them. React.js was used in the development of this application. ReactStrap was used for all styling.

Kalis



An Electronic Medical Record(EMR) meant to create, store, and view patient data. In an industry where EMR's can take weeks to learn how to use properly, Kalis is a user friendly application where creating and viewing patient data is made easy. This is a full stack application that utilizes React.js and a Django Rest API.
